Dachshund Name FAQs

What are good names for a dachshund?

Great dachshund names lean into their German heritage or long, sausage-like shape. Popular choices include Pretzel, Noodle, Schnitzel, Bratwurst, Fritz, Heidi, and Greta. Food-inspired names like Churro, Strudel, and Wurst are also fan favorites.

What are funny dachshund names?

Funny dachshund names play on their long body and short legs. Try Bark Twain, Lady Wigglebottom, Torpedo, Slinky, Shorty, Wiggles, or Noodle for a laugh every time you call them.

What are German dachshund names?

Classic German names for dachshunds include Fritz, Hans, Greta, Heidi, Liesl, Otto, Kaiser, Bruno, and Bratwurst. These names honor the breed's German origins (Dachshund literally means "badger dog" in German).
